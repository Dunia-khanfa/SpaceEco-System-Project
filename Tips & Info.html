Operational Guidelines & Protocols

Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) for waste handling and environmental safety aboard the ISS.

Water Recovery System (WRS)

Critical for station sustainability.

  • Condensate: Ensure air conditioning vents are clear to capture humidity.
  • Urine Processing: Do not flush chemicals or wipes into the liquid waste system.
  • Leaks: Report any free-floating liquid immediately to prevent circuit damage.
Hazardous Materials

Batteries, Chemicals, and Biologicals.

  • Batteries: Tape terminals before disposal to prevent short circuits and fires.
  • Chemical Wipes: Seal in "Ziploc-Double" bags to contain fumes.
  • Sharps: Use the rigid red containers only. Never force items in.
