Category 01

Wet Waste & Food

Organic waste poses a biological hazard in closed environments. All food scraps and wet wipes must be sealed in Green-Label moisture-proof bags (K-BAR) immediately to prevent bacterial growth and odors.

Double-seal required
Category 02

Dry Waste & Packaging

Packaging materials, paper, and foam must be flattened to conserve volume. Use the Manual Compactor in Node 2 before placing items in the Blue-Label bins.

Category 03 - Critical

Hazardous Materials

Batteries, chemical wipes, and unknown fluids are classified as hazardous. These must be transported immediately to the specialized containment unit in Laboratory Sector 4.

Category 04

Electronic Waste

Broken PCBs, wires, and sensors. Store small electronics in anti-static bags. Large components must be logged in the inventory system and secured in the cargo bay.

Hazardous Materials Protocol
Standard Operating Procedure:
  • Wear protective gloves and eye-gear before handling.
  • Do not open leaking containers.
  • Place item in a double-sealed Red Bin.
  • Log the specific chemical or component type in the "Log Waste" page.
  • Notify Mission Control if a spill occurs.
